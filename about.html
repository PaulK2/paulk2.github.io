About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary ITS Delivery Specialist and Systems Engineer passionate about connecting technology, communication, and design. I’ve worked with enterprise environments at A1, Bosch, and HP, gaining a broad technical foundation across IT systems, hardware, and client coordination.

My career started in hands-on hardware assembly, progressed through IT support, and grew into system delivery. I’ve learned that real success comes from communication between people — not just perfect code or infrastructure.

I studied Technical Engineering at the Technical University of Berlin (4 semesters) before continuing my growth in full-time roles, where I discovered how to combine engineering with creativity and teamwork.

I’m now focused on international technical delivery roles in the UK, combining analytical thinking, cross-cultural communication, and hands-on system experience.
